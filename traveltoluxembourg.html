How Saarland University Students Can Travel to Luxembourg for Free with the mKaart

Abdul Habeeb 4 mins to read

As a student at Saarland University, you have an amazing opportunity to explore Luxembourg without breaking the bank. Thanks to the mKaart with the "Studentenabo," students under 30 years old can enjoy free travel on the CFL Saarbrücken-Luxemburg Express bus and local transport in Luxembourg. Here’s a comprehensive guide on how to apply for and use this fantastic benefit.

Since March 1, 2020, students of Saarland University have enjoyed a remarkable benefit: free travel to Luxembourg via bus using their semester travel ticket. This initiative allows students to commute seamlessly between Saarbrücken and Luxembourg without incurring any transportation costs on the CFL Saarbrücken-Luxemburg Express operated by Emile Weber .

Once in Luxembourg, this privilege extends to free usage of public transport, including buses, trains, and trams throughout the Grand Duchy. This comprehensive access not only enhances mobility but also encourages students to explore Luxembourg’s cultural and historical treasures conveniently and affordably.
